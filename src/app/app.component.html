Counter Service

countmatic.io provides webservices to count stuff.

We keep your numbers save, give you increments, states and resets. All of this as service without any registration

Get started with the webservice that counts

3 minutes to get started with our service

Request a new counter

PUT https://api.countmatic.io/counter?name=MyCounter 

Result: {'token':'ehdu75gdj#98hjdt'}

Get the current number of your counter

GET https://api.countmatic.io/counter?token=ehdu75gdj#98hjdt 

Result: {'MyCounter':'0'}

Increment your counter

POST https://api.countmatic.io/counter?token=ehdu75gdj#98hjdt 

Result: {'MyCounter':'1'}

Delete your counter

DELETE https://api.countmatic.io/counter?token=ehdu75gdj#98hjdt 

Result: {'MyCounter':'0'}
